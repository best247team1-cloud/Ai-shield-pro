How to Use AI Shield

Mastering this tool is simple. Follow these steps to ensure your data remains private while getting the best results from AI models like ChatGPT or Gemini.

Step 1: Input Your Draft

Paste your raw text, email draft, or rough question into the main input box.

Step 2: Choose Your Mode

  • Redact Privacy: Click the RED button if your text contains sensitive info (Emails, Phone Numbers). The tool will replace them with placeholders like [EMAIL_REDACTED].
  • Polish Prompt: Click the BLUE button if you want to enhance the quality of your request. The tool wraps your text in a "Persona-based" structure.

Step 3: Use Helper Tools

Use the top toolbar to clean extra spaces, check reading time, or convert your text format before copying.
